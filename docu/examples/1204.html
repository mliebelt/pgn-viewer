#6: locale in edit mode

Javascript part

var pgn = '1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 Nc6  3.Bc4 Bc5';
pgnEdit('board', {pgn: pgn, locale: 'fr'});
pgnEdit('board1', {pgn: pgn,locale: 'de'});
pgnEdit('board2', {pgn: pgn, locale: 'en'});

HTML part

See ticket 6 at GitHub. The boards above have the three locales fr, de, and en, and show the moves initially in the language of choice. However, when you edit the games, you will see that the last locale (here english) is used in all of them.
